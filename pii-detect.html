Why use PII Cloak?

While frameworks like Microsoft Presidio are powerful and highly customizable, they often require integration effort and infrastructure. PII Cloak is designed as a ready-to-use API that delivers fast PII detection with minimal setup, allowing teams to integrate privacy protection in minutes instead of days. Further, we help you with quick integrations into your data pipeline in a variety of deployment models.
